## What Exactly is a Notary Public in California?

A notary public is more than just an official stamp—they're a state-appointed guardian of document integrity. In California, these professionals serve as impartial witnesses who:

- Verify individual identities
- Prevent document fraud
- Authenticate signatures
- Administer legal oaths and affirmations

### Legal Requirements: The California Standard

California maintains rigorous standards for notary professionals:

1. Minimum age: 18 years
2. California residency
